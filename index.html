About Me

Hello! My name is Chris Vincent and I'm an aspiring coder! I was born and raised in Maryland and moved to Florida in 2015. I've worked in the hospitality industry for 8 years, and I'm excited to make a change.

I like to classify myself as a diligent worker, who does anything and everything to be the best possible version of myself. When I have difficulty doing something, it will stir in my head until I can find a solution. I love problem solving when it comes to fixing things!

In my free time I enjoy playing video games, playing slow-pitch softball, and going to amusement parks. I've always been passionate about playing and watching sports, especially baseball. My favorite sports teams are the Washington Redskins, Washington Wizards, and the Baltimore Orioles.
